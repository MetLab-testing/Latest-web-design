About MetLab

MetLab Testing and Analysis Services is a Qatari-owned materials testing laboratory specializing in Corrosion, Mechanical, Coating, and Metallurgical analysis.

Our work aligns with the Qatar National Vision 2030, promoting industrial self-reliance, safety, and sustainability.

With precision, integrity, and technical excellence, we serve oil & gas, petrochemical, marine, and manufacturing industries.
